Challenges that workforces are grappling with:

The ongoing economic uncertainty is bringing massive changes across industries and workplaces. And enormous shifts are predicted in how we work to what skills we will need.

In this environment, it is crucial that we identify and are successfully able to predict emerging skill gaps, and how we can stay relevant in the future.

Our solution “Careers Tomorrow” empowers the workforce to receive guidance on their current skills, recommend the skills needed to remain relevant in the future, explore career paths aligned to suitable learning and job opportunities.

The app serves as a digital career coach that uses augmented analytics to provide the user the opportunity to:

  • Understand skills, performance achievement, potential, and aspirations
  • Contextualized skill gap analysis for current and aspired roles
  • Provide career recommendations for today and tomorrow
  • View Skills Trends and Competencies required for future
  • Get actionable recommendations on career paths and Technical/Behavioural assessments
  • Personalized Learning Recommendations and Timely Nudges
  • Virtual Community for Knowledge sharing and learning

The app is designed to help individuals as well as enterprises to improve lifelong employability through skills upgrading and continuous learning. The Careers Tomorrow App is available on iOS App and Google Play Store - LINK

How to remain Future Ready and Relevant?
